Company: Stripe

Stage: Series A

Industry: Fintech / Payments Infrastructure

Business Model: API-based transaction processing. Stripe generates revenue by charging a percentage fee on every successful payment processed through its platform.

------------------------------------------------------------
COMPANY OVERVIEW
------------------------------------------------------------

Stripe is a financial infrastructure company that provides developers and internet businesses with APIs to accept, manage, and optimize online payments. The company focuses on simplifying payment integration for startups and software companies by replacing complex legacy payment systems with a modern, developer-first platform.

Founded in 2010 by brothers Patrick and John Collison, Stripe's mission is to increase the GDP of the internet by making online commerce easier to build and operate.

------------------------------------------------------------
PROBLEM
------------------------------------------------------------

Accepting online payments has historically been difficult. Existing payment processors require lengthy onboarding, outdated APIs, manual underwriting, and significant engineering effort.

Developers often spend weeks integrating payment systems while managing fraud prevention, PCI compliance, banking relationships, and international payment regulations.

Small businesses and startups are underserved by enterprise-focused payment providers.

------------------------------------------------------------
SOLUTION
------------------------------------------------------------

Stripe provides a developer-friendly payment platform with simple REST APIs, comprehensive documentation, software development kits, and infrastructure that allows businesses to begin accepting payments in hours rather than weeks.

The platform abstracts payment complexity including:

- Card processing
- Payment authorization
- Fraud detection
- PCI compliance
- International payments
- Recurring billing
- Developer tooling
